The Missing Piece

NTTA has the detection. GoCarma has the delivery.

NTTA already operates a 24/7 Safety Operations Center monitoring ~2,000 roadside cameras, has deployed thermal imaging cameras for wrong-way driver detection, and runs Roadside Safety Services with a 17-minute average response time.

But the entire system is infrastructure-based and reactive: cameras detect a problem → SOC dispatches responders → overhead signs warn nearby drivers.

The gap: no direct-to-driver channel. When NTTA detects a wrong-way driver, the alert chain is SOC → DPS → overhead message boards. GoCarma can push that alert directly to every driver on the corridor via CarPlay/Android Auto in seconds — before they ever see the overhead board. Same for active work zones, incidents, and ice closures.

NTTA is investing in detection but has no last-mile delivery to the driver's phone or dashboard. GoCarma's 100K users are that last mile.

Eligibility

NTTA qualifies to apply — independently.

NTTA is a political subdivision of the State of Texas under Chapter 366 of the Transportation Code, created by the Texas Legislature (SB 370, 1997). This makes NTTA a qualifying applicant under the SS4A "political subdivision" category.

NCTCOG, the regional MPO, already has a qualifying Safety Action Plan funded by $9M in prior SS4A grants. Under program rules, NTTA can reference NCTCOG's plan without needing NCTCOG to be the applicant. This is a standard provision: any entity within an MPO's boundary can use the MPO's Action Plan.

NCTCOG's safety work is almost entirely focused on pedestrian and bike safety on local roads. Nobody has addressed the 1,000+ miles of toll and managed lanes in the region. NTTA fills that gap.

The Concept

A Planning & Demonstration Grant to pilot connected driver safety.

NTTA applies for $1–2M to pilot a connected driver safety system on the toll network, powered by GoCarma's existing user base.

  • $
    Real-time TEXpress Lanes pricing — a CarPlay/Android Auto feature that keeps 100K commuters actively engaged every day
  • !
    Safety notifications layered on top — work zone warnings, incident alerts, wrong-way driver notifications, ice/closure alerts
  • ✓
    Before/after safety evaluation — measuring driver behavior changes and incident reduction
  • ☷
    Zero roadside infrastructure — the 100K phones already on the network are the infrastructure

The pricing feature is GoCarma's commercial product (not grant-funded). The grant funds the safety layer built on top of an already-engaged user base — solving the adoption problem that kills most safety tech pilots.

Unlocking TEXpress Pricing Data

Currently no one has real-time access to TEXpress pricing. Toll operators (Transcore, Cintra) calculate prices roadside and push them to NTTA after the fact — NTTA receives the prices but has no independent verification or consumer-facing display. Through this partnership, NTTA would share the pricing feed with GoCarma to power the app. This gives NTTA an independent audit trail of pricing per segment, and gives GoCarma the real-time pricing data needed — without the millions the toll operators would charge for a direct API.
